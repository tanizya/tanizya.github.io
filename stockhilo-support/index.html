Stock HiLo Support

About

Stock HiLo is a stock prediction app. Swipe up or down on real stock charts to predict High or Low. Vote on 10 stocks, then see how the community voted.

Contact

If you have any questions, feedback, or issues, please open an issue on GitHub.

Privacy Policy

Stock HiLo does not collect any personal information. The app uses anonymous voting data to display community predictions.

Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

This app uses the App Tracking Transparency framework to request your permission before any tracking occurs. You can change your tracking preference at any time in Settings.
